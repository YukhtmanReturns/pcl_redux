Patientschecklist.com strives to help patients empower themselves. We believe the best person to provide you with answers to your questions is your own physician. The founders of Patientschecklist.com are two average guys that realized healthcare can be made better in simple, yet effective ways. We have a team of nurses, nurse practitioners, and physician specialists who contribute questions to our database. We also hope you will share your experiences with your fellow users on our forum, or by adding your own questions and sharing them with us. If you had a bad experience, help others avoid those same pitfalls.

We are all patients at some point in our lives: let’s make healthcare better together.


Aperçu Inc.

Aperçu Inc. owns the website Patientschecklist.com. Our goal is to create products that are straightforward and easy-to-use that will help enhance the average person's experience with healthcare. We believe in equal access to quality healthcare, and that by being a better informed patient, everyone involved will benefit.
